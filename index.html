About Us


E-CUE ASSOCIATES LTD is an Environmental Consultancy Firm, specializing in all spheres of environmental interpretation, analysis, planning and management. The firm is incorporated in the Republic of Kenya in 2009 and is registered with the National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) as a Firm of Experts and is a corporate member of the Environment Institute of Kenya (EIK).
