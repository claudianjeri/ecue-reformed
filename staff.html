Staff

Home Staff

Tom Omenda

Executive Director and has been with the company since its inception in 2009. He has developed over 25 years’ experience in environmental management consultancy for a wide range of infrastructure projects in Kenya, Sudan, Tanzania and Uganda, including roads infrastructure, power transmission lines, dams, land-use change, urban development projects, water and sanitation and urban planning. Most of the projects that Mr. Omenda has successfully undertaken have been funded by International Development Partners such as the World Bank, African Development Bank, AFD (Agence Française de Développement), EU, EIB and JICA. He holds a Master’s degree in Ecology from the University of Toronto, Canada, a Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Nairobi and a post graduate diploma from Dresden University of Technology, Germany. He is registered as a Lead Environmental Impact Assessor & Auditor by National Environment Management Authority (NEMA) and is a member of the Environment Institute of Kenya (EIK).

Sellah Odera

Environmentalist with 7 years’ experience. She is registered with NEMA as an associate expert, holds a Bachelor of Science degree from Moi University and a Master’s degree in Environmental studies (Environmental Health). She has experience in conducting Environmental Impact assessments, Strategic Environmental Assessments, Environmental audits and supervision of ongoing projects with a bias towards health and safety compliance monitoring. She also has experience in conducting research in Environmental health related disciplines. Sellah is registered by the Kenya National Environmental Management Authority (NEMA) as an Associate Expert and a member of the Environment Institute of Kenya.

Julie Sewe

Registered with NEMA as an associate expert with two years’ experience. She holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Agroecosystems and Environmental Management and is currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Environmental Planning and management. Sewe has participated in conducting environmental and social impact assessment (ESIA) studies for diverse projects ranging from water structures, roads, railway and commercial properties. Her consultancy work has also involved development of environmental master plans, conducting strategic environmental assessment (SEA) and supervision of construction works.

Verah Goko

Environmentalist with 3 years’ experience in environmental impact assessment and auditing for various development projects, across multiple sectors. She is a registered Environmental Impact Assessment and Auditing Associate Expert and a member of the Environmental institute of Kenya. She has participated in conducting environmental and social impact assessment (ESIA) studies for diverse projects ranging from, water structures, roads, and commercial properties. Vera’s consultancy work has also involved development of environmental master plans and conducting strategic environmental assessment (SEA) and supervision of construction works.

Evans Otieno

E-cue Associates Ltd
